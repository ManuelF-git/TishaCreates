As a Writer

I've been writing since I was fourteen years old. Sitting at a little table in a back room of my parents house by candle light. The pull of Storytelling has always been strong, dreaming of the alternate endings of a storyline. After years of struggling to figure out how to get those stories from my imagination, to page, I had learned the ways to get them to print.

As a Speaker/Motivator

Since I was a teen, I've spoken out against social injustices. I've always been one to stand up for marginalized and underrepresented groups. After some of my own experiences,my focus is now geared towards those men and women who feel stuck, suffering in silence at the hands of those who are supposed to love them the most


As a Lifecoach

I am so comitted to doing this thing called life with you. There are a lot of us smiling through the pain, posting the happy side of us to social media while hiding the other side of us far away, allow me to enter that space with you, speak to you and get you ready to take charge of your life.
